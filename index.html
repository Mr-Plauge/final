About Me

Hello! I am Owyn Lee, an aspiring technical artist. I have been self-learning programming and digital art since 2018. My hobbies include modding games, deconstructing rigs and shaders, and collaborating with others to fulfill creative visions. My portfolio includes the projects I have developed, as well as an overview of my contributions to the modding community.
